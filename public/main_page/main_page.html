About the Website

The "EasyFind" website helps people find available bikes to rent or buy near them. They will see all available bike stores in the city or can choose a specific store on the map. Each store will have some description and information about available bikes and their prices

Find available Bike Stores

To see available places hover over 'Locations' in the navigation bar and choose the desired city.

Join Us

If bike store owner want to be show on our website, they can go to 'Join' section in the navigation bar. After filling the contact form our administrator will get in touch with you to discuss further details.

As of now, 10 locations from 5 locations are presented on our Website and the number is constantly growing.
